Ball Moss: The Monk

Ball Moss are often found dangling from trees, suspended acrobatically among the branches. They collect water from the air and make their own food.

Scientific Name: Tillandsia recurvata

Lifespan: 15+ years

Native to: Southern North America


Experienced monks gain the ability to run up walls and on water. They are also generally known for dexterous feats and acrobatics.
